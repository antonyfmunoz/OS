# Skill: Analyze Sales Conversation

## Purpose

Analyze a DM conversation and determine the best next response.

The goal is to move the conversation toward identifying pain and booking a call.

---

## Inputs

Conversation log located in:

03_CRM/Conversations

---

## Analysis

Identify:

• emotional signals
• frustration level
• self-awareness
• readiness for change

---

## Conversation Stage

Determine which stage the conversation is in:

1. Opening
2. Pain discovery
3. Problem reframing
4. Offer introduction
5. Call invitation

---

## Generate Response

Provide:

• recommended next message
• follow-up question
• conversation strategy

---

## Output

Append recommendations to the conversation file.
