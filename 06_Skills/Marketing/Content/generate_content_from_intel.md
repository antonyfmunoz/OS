# Skill: Generate Content From Market Intelligence

## Purpose

Turn market intelligence reports into high-performing content ideas that mirror the language and psychology of the ICP.

---

## Outcome

A dated content ideas file saved to `09_Content/Content_Ideas/` — containing hooks, reel/TikTok ideas, carousel ideas, long-form topics, and discussion prompts grounded in real ICP language.

---

## Best-Practice Benchmark

Every idea must trace back to an actual ICP insight or language pattern from the intelligence. If you can't cite the source insight, the idea is invented, not researched.

---

## Decision Criteria

- Generate hooks if: targeting short-form engagement and DM conversion
- Generate reel/TikTok ideas if: video content is planned for this cycle
- Generate carousel ideas if: educational format is being used this week
- Generate long-form topics if: YouTube or livestream is planned
- Generate discussion prompts if: comment engagement is the goal

---

## Execution Steps

1. Load market intelligence reports from: `07_Knowledge/Reports/Market_Reports`
2. Extract: frustrations, fears, desires, identity conflicts, language patterns
3. Generate 10 short-form hooks
   - Example format: "You don't lack motivation. You lack structure."
4. Generate 5 short-form video topics based on ICP pain patterns
5. Generate 3 educational carousel topics
6. Generate 3 YouTube / livestream topics
7. Generate 5 discussion prompts designed to provoke comments from the ICP
8. Save to: `09_Content/Content_Ideas/content_ideas_YYYY-MM-DD.md`

---

## Failure Modes

- Generating ideas not grounded in actual ICP data (invented frustrations)
- Hooks that could apply to any audience — must be specific to the ICP
- Topics that explain the offer instead of creating curiosity or addressing pain
- Saving ideas without a date — makes pattern tracking impossible

---

## Measurement

- Idea-to-use rate: % of generated ideas published within 14 days
- Engagement rate on content built directly from this output
- Hook performance: which idea categories consistently outperform

---

## Improvement Opportunities

- Tag each idea with the ICP insight it's based on to close the feedback loop
- Retire idea categories that consistently underperform
- Build a swipe file of the top-performing hooks from each intelligence cycle
