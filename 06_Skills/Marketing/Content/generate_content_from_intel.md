# Skill: Generate Content From Market Intelligence

## Purpose

Turn market intelligence reports into high-performing content ideas.

The goal is to mirror the language and psychology of the ICP.

---

## Inputs

Market intelligence reports located in:

07_Knowledge/Reports/Market_Reports

---

## Analysis

Extract:

• frustrations  
• fears  
• desires  
• identity conflicts  
• language patterns

---

## Generate Content Types

### Hooks

Generate 10 short-form hooks.

Example format:

"You don't lack motivation. You lack structure."

---

### Reels / TikTok Ideas

Generate 5 short-form video topics based on ICP pain patterns.

---

### Carousel Ideas

Generate 3 educational carousel topics.

---

### Long Form Content

Generate 3 YouTube / livestream topics.

---

### Discussion Prompts

Generate 5 questions designed to provoke comments from the ICP.

---

## Output Location

Save content ideas to:

09_Content/Content_Ideas

File format:

content_ideas_YYYY_MM_DD.md
