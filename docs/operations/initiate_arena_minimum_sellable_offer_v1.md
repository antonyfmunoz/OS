## Offer Name

**Initiate Arena** — Founding Cohort

## Who It Is For

Men aged 18–35 who:
- Know what they should be doing but cannot execute consistently
- Have consumed self-improvement content without applying it
- Feel stuck between their potential and their output
- Are ready to invest time, money, and effort in a structured transformation
- Want accountability, not more information

---

## Who It Is NOT For

- People who just want free content or motivation
- People who argue about whether discipline matters
- People who aren't ready to invest (time or money)
- People looking for passive consumption (videos, PDFs, courses)
- People who are defensive about feedback or structure
- Bots, engagement pods, and tire-kickers

---

## Core Promise

You will go from knowing what to do to actually doing it — every day — for 90 days. You will come out with proof of your own execution ability that no one can take from you.

---

## Mechanism

**Structure + accountability over 90 days.**

This is not a course. There are no videos to binge. This is a training camp. You show up, you follow the structure, you execute, and you are held accountable the entire way.

The system makes the right action the default action. You stop relying on motivation and start relying on structure.
