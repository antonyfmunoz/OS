| 1 | "Where are you at with execution right now? Honestly." | Pain intensity | Self-diagnosis. "I'm fine" = not qualified. Gap admission = qualified. |

## 11. Objection Responses

> DRAFT_READY_FOR_FOUNDER_VOICE_REVIEW

### "I can't afford it"

> I hear that. And I'm not going to pressure you. But I'll ask this — what's the cost of another year stuck where you are? Another year of knowing what to do and not doing it? At $750, that's less than $9 a day for 90 days of structure, accountability, and real change. If this is the problem, the cost of not solving it is always bigger than the cost of solving it. But it's your call.

### "I don't have time"

> That's actually the exact problem this solves. The daily commitment is 30–60 minutes — less than the time most people spend scrolling. Initiate Arena doesn't add to your plate. It restructures how you use the time you already have. If you had the structure to prioritize and execute, "I don't have time" stops being true.

### "I've tried programs before"

> Respect that. And I'd bet most of those programs gave you information — videos, PDFs, modules. This isn't that. Initiate Arena is a training camp. There's daily execution, real accountability, and a brotherhood that doesn't let you slide. The reason past programs didn't work isn't because you failed. It's because they were built for information delivery, not execution training.

### "I can figure it out myself"

> Maybe. But if figuring it out yourself was going to work, wouldn't it have worked by now? The people who come to me have been figuring it out themselves for months or years and they're still in the same place. The gap isn't intelligence or capability. It's structure and accountability. That's what this provides.

### "What makes this different?"

> Two things. First — it's not information. You don't need more knowledge. You need a system that makes you execute. That's what this is. Second — you come out with proof. Not a certificate. 90 days of execution data that shows you can follow through. That changes how you see yourself. That's permanent.

### "I need to think about it"

> Take the time you need. But I'll say this — thinking about it is exactly the pattern we're talking about. You already know if this is a problem. The question is whether today's the day you decide to solve it or whether you think about it for another week and end up in the same place. No pressure. When you're ready, I'm here.
