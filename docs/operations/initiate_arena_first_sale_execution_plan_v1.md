## Prospecting Criteria

### Where to Find Prospects

| Source | How | Priority |
|--------|-----|----------|
| Comments on your own posts | People engaging with your content already — warmest leads | HIGH |
| Comments on adjacent creator posts | People interested in discipline, self-improvement, execution | HIGH |
| Engaged followers who haven't been contacted | People who like/save/share but haven't been DMed | HIGH |
| DM requests and story replies | People reaching out proactively | HIGHEST |
| Followers of competitors | People already in the market for transformation | MEDIUM |
| Hashtag searches (#discipline, #executionoverexcuse, etc.) | Cold but targeted | LOW |

### Prospect Signals (Green Flags)

- Posts about wanting to change, be more disciplined, stop procrastinating
- Comments expressing frustration with their own consistency
- Content about starting something new, getting back on track
- Engagement with self-improvement content creators
- Story shares of motivational content with personal captions

### Disqualification Signals (Red Flags)

- Only posts memes / reposted content with no personal engagement
- Argues about whether discipline is real or important
- Profile suggests bot or engagement pod
- Immediately asks about price before any conversation
- Defensive or hostile in comments

## DM / Comment Workflow

```
FIND PROSPECT
    ↓
ENGAGE (like their content, leave genuine comment)
    ↓
DM OPENER (never pitch in first message)
    ↓
CONVERSATION (2–5 messages)
    ↓
QUALIFY (use 3-of-5 framework)
    ↓
    ├── NOT QUALIFIED → Nurture (stay friendly, no pitch)
    └── QUALIFIED → Book call or present offer in DM
            ↓
            ├── CALL BOOKED → Run call → Close or follow up
            └── DM CLOSE → Send payment link → Onboard
```
