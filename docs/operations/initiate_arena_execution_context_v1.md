## 4. DM Conversation Framework

### Rules

1. Never pitch in message one
2. Open a genuine conversation about their situation
3. Listen more than talk
4. Qualification happens naturally through conversation
5. The offer is presented only after qualification
6. Every DM must be approved by founder before sending

### Conversation arc

```
OPENER (genuine, no pitch)
    ↓
CONVERSATION (2–5 messages, understand their situation)
    ↓
QUALIFY (3-of-5 framework, naturally embedded)
    ↓
├── NOT QUALIFIED → Nurture (stay friendly, no pitch)
└── QUALIFIED → Present offer or book call
        ↓
        ├── CALL → 5-phase / 20-min structure → close or follow up
        └── DM CLOSE → payment link → onboard
```

### Opener variants (4)

| Variant | Context | Opening move |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| A — Value-first | Warm lead, saw their content | Reference specific content + ask about execution gap |
| B — Engagement response | They commented on Antony's content | Acknowledge comment + ask about their execution |
| C — Direct/cold | Found via hashtag or adjacent creator | Reference their topic + ask if execution is a gap |
| D — Story reply | They posted a story | Genuine response to story + ask about execution level |

### Follow-up cadence

| Trigger | Timing | Approach |
|---------|--------|----------|
| No response | 3 days | Casual check-in, no pressure |
| Interest shown | Same day | Suggest call or explain more |
| Call booked | Immediately | Confirm details, set expectations |
| After call, no close | Same day | Respect + payment link + door open |
| After payment | Within 1 hour | Confirmation + onboarding details |

## 5. Qualification Rubric

### 3-of-5 framework

| # | Criterion | Question to ask | Green signal | Red signal |
|---|-----------|----------------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Pain intensity | "Where are you at with execution right now? Honestly." | Self-diagnosis, gap admission | "I'm fine" / denial |
| 2 | Urgency | "If something actually worked, when would you want to start?" | "Now" / "this week" | "Someday" / vague |
| 3 | Willingness to change | "What have you tried to fix it?" | Past effort, asks about solutions | "Nothing" / low engagement |
| 4 | Coachability | "Are you open to following a structured system even when it's uncomfortable?" | "Yes" + curiosity | Pushback / defensiveness |
| 5 | Ability to invest | "If this is the right fit, are you in a position to invest in yourself right now?" | Open discussion | Immediate shutdown |

**Threshold**: 3 of 5 green signals = qualified
**Action**: Qualified → present offer or book call. Not qualified → nurture.

## 6. Objection Map

| Objection | Core concern | Response strategy |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| "I can't afford it" | Price barrier | Reframe: cost of inaction > cost of action. $8.33/day for 90 days. |
| "I don't have time" | Perceived time investment | Reframe: 30–60 min/day, less than scrolling. Restructures existing time. |
| "I've tried programs before" | Past failure, skepticism | Distinguish: training camp ≠ information course. Accountability ≠ modules. |
| "I can figure it out myself" | Self-reliance / pride | Gentle challenge: if self-solving worked, it would have worked by now. |
| "What makes this different?" | Differentiation need | Two points: (1) execution not information, (2) 90-day proof as permanent outcome. |
| "I need to think about it" | Decision avoidance | Name the pattern: thinking about it IS the pattern. No pressure, door open. |
