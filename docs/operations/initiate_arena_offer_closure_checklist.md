## Decision Packet

The founder needs to make these decisions to unblock the first sale. Each has recommended defaults.

| # | Decision | Recommended Default | Why |
|---|----------|-------------------|-----|
| 1 | Price | $750 (pay-in-full) or $297 × 3 (payment plan) | Aligns with `CLAUDE.local.md` reference, sustainable unit economics (~14 sales/month for $10K target) |
| 2 | Payment method | Stripe payment link | Zero setup friction, professional, instant confirmation |
| 3 | Call booking | Calendly free tier | Referenced in workflow docs, free, immediate |
| 4 | Delivery container | Discord server (private) | Referenced in workflow docs, free, community-capable |
| 5 | Enrollment type | Rolling (not cohort) for MVP | Lower complexity, sell anytime, no "wait for next cohort" friction |
| 6 | Format | Group + 1:1 check-ins | Group community + weekly 1:1 accountability. Scales better than pure 1:1, more valuable than pure group. |
| 7 | Refund policy | 7-day money-back, no questions | Reduces buyer risk without unlimited exposure. Standard in this market. |
| 8 | CRM | Google Sheet | Zero setup cost, immediate, upgradeable later |

**Note**: These are recommendations, not decisions. The founder makes the final call on each.
