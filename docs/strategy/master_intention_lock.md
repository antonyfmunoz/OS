## Physical Production + Manufacturing Sovereignty

The empire should evolve beyond software, media, and services into owned physical production, manufacturing, fulfillment, robotics, and proprietary component capabilities.

**Progression**:
- Consumer of tools → orchestrator of tools
- Owner of proprietary software → owner of proprietary workflows
- Owner of proprietary products → owner of physical production
- Owner of manufacturing/fulfillment infrastructure
- Owner of robotics/automation systems
- Owner of capital allocation and operating intelligence

**Anti-Copycat Moat Doctrine**: In an AI-native copycat economy, product features are easily replicated. Physical infrastructure, manufacturing capability, supply chain ownership, proprietary components, operating history, and execution data are not. See also: Physical Infrastructure Moat Doctrine, Physical Product Intelligence Layer.

**Custom Actuator / Robotics Component Doctrine**: Buy commodity actuator and component parts. Customize strategic parts. Own bottleneck parts. Manufacture moat-critical parts. CNC machining, prototyping, and custom component manufacturing only where ownership creates advantage. Do not begin by manufacturing everything from scratch.

**Timeline**: Physical production capabilities activate after digital companies generate revenue and capital. Sequence: first revenue → portfolio revenue → vertical integration → manufacturing → fulfillment → robotics. Each stage funds the next.
