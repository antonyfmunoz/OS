5. Physical product sovereignty and manufacturing intelligence

Without these doctrines locked, the War Sprint could build architecture that blocks or ignores these future capabilities. The amendment ensures the sprint designs with extensibility in mind.

## New Doctrines Added (7)

### 1. Parallel Operating Cell Doctrine
UMH/EOS must eventually support multiple companies, agents, simulations, research loops, worldbuilding loops, training agents, family office agents, and operating cells running in parallel. Each cell requires typed scope, mission, authority level, inputs, outputs, cadence, owner, tools, KPIs, memory policy, reporting format, and governance boundaries. Core rule: parallelize exploration, centralize truth, govern execution, integrate learning, compound outputs. 11 future cell types defined. Early cells read-only/advisory first.

### 2. Always-On Intelligence Doctrine
Research, worldbuilding, market intelligence, training, portfolio analysis, content intelligence, and simulation loops should eventually run continuously in background. Always-on means persistent observation, research, synthesis, and recommendation under governance — not autonomous execution.

### 3. Algorithmic Self-Modeling Doctrine
UMH should observe user's recommendation feeds, saved media, liked content, and consumption patterns to reconstruct an algorithmic mirror of interests, tastes, market awareness, aesthetic preferences, and attention loops. Goal is inferring the user's personal algorithmic profile, not reverse-engineering platform algorithms. Preserves source, timestamp, platform, sensitivity, and review status.

### 4. Multimodal Course / Content Assimilation Doctrine
UMH should eventually consume courses, videos, podcasts, books, documents, screenshots, whiteboards, notes, lectures, social posts, and media content — assimilating into goals, companies, products, memory, templates, libraries, and execution plans. Perception (watching, reading, extracting, summarizing) is not execution (clicking, posting, sending, buying, editing, deleting).

### 5. Physical Product Sovereignty Doctrine
UMH should help transform references, images, sketches, products, and concepts into prototype-ready product intelligence: 3D models, CAD briefs, BOMs, manufacturing constraints, supplier requirements, test plans, and robotics/manufacturing workflows. AI-generated 3D mesh is not manufacturing-ready CAD. Manufacturing decisions require validation, dimensions, tolerances, materials, stress checks, compliance, cost modeling, and supplier constraints.

### 6. Human Evaluation + Negotiation Intelligence Doctrine
UMH/EOS should assist evaluating advisors, operators, vendors, manufacturers, partners, consultants, agencies, and hires by extracting claims, evidence, incentives, risks, contradictions, negotiation leverage, and follow-up questions. Must avoid unsupported psychological diagnosis or protected-class inference. Must preserve legal, consent, privacy, and compliance boundaries.

### 7. Family Office / Capital Intelligence Doctrine
UMH should eventually support portfolio intelligence, deal analysis, asset evaluation, risk modeling, acquisition theses, capital allocation scenarios, and family-office-style advisory workflows. Early versions advisory-only. Human approval required for all financial actions. No autonomous real-money trading, capital allocation, or investment execution.
