# Workbook Framework — Design Philosophy

## What It Is
A methodology for building curriculum
and lead magnets simultaneously.

NOT a built product.
A design philosophy for how to build.

## The Framework
Every lesson in the program requires:
  1. A learning component (the lesson)
  2. An assignment (apply the learning)
  3. A workbook (tool to complete the assignment)

## Workbook Design Rules
Workbooks teach: WHAT, WHEN, WHY
Workbooks do NOT teach: HOW

The HOW is reserved for the paid program.
This creates natural upgrade motivation.

## Workbook as Lead Magnet
Each workbook maps to a specific pain point
of the Initiate Arena offer.

Example:
  Pain point: "I start things but never finish"
  Workbook: "The Completion Framework"
  Teaches: what completion requires,
           when to apply it, why it matters
  Does not teach: the actual system (that's in IA)
  Result: prospect experiences value,
          feels the gap,
          wants the HOW
