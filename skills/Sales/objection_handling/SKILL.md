# Skill: Objection Handling

## Purpose

Identify and respond to the specific objections that prevent Initiate Arena leads from booking or closing.

---

## Outcome

A response that addresses the real fear behind the stated objection, reframes it, and moves the conversation toward a decision.

---

## Best-Practice Benchmark

The real objection is never the stated one. "I can't afford it" = fear of wasting money. "I need to think about it" = not enough certainty yet. Handle the fear, not the words.

---

## Decision Criteria

- What is the stated objection?
- What is the likely real fear beneath it?
- What evidence would resolve that fear?
- Is this a real objection or a brush-off?

---

## Execution Steps

1. Acknowledge the objection without resistance
2. Ask a clarifying question to surface the real fear
3. Reframe using their own language
4. Provide specific evidence or social proof
5. Return to the decision point

---

## Failure Modes

- Arguing against the stated objection
- Giving more information instead of asking
- Not acknowledging the objection first
- Pushing after two clear nos

---

## Measurement

- Objection-to-booking conversion rate
- Which objections convert most often
- Which objections signal unqualified leads

---

## Improvement Opportunities

Build objection library from real DM data. Track which responses convert vs which lose.
