# ICP Signal Detection

Patterns for identifying ideal customer profile signals from social media and direct conversations. Used to qualify leads and craft outreach.

## Core Framework

Signals are captured in `01_Inbox/` as raw or processed entries, then analyzed into structured insight pages in `07_Knowledge/ICP/`. Each signal maps to a pain pattern, psychological state, and language pattern.

## Archetypes

### Ambitious but Stuck

- **Pain pattern:** Time blindness meets behavior drift
- **Psychology:** Has identity ("I'm capable") but no system connecting identity to behavior
- **Key language:** "I feel like I'm capable of more", "every week just disappears"
- **ICP match:** High — already self-diagnosed, needs structure not motivation
- **Source:** [[07_Knowledge/ICP/ambitious_but_stuck_time_blindness]]

### Frustrated Drifter

- **Pain pattern:** Chronic project abandonment, shiny object syndrome
- **Psychology:** High-energy, curious, but abandons when excitement fades
- **Key language:** "I keep starting", "never finishing", "wasting my life"
- **ICP match:** High — identity-level pain, high urgency
- **Source:** [[07_Knowledge/ICP/frustrated_drifter_project_abandonment]]

## Signal Analysis Pipeline

1. Raw signals land in `01_Inbox/raw_signals/`
2. Processed into `01_Inbox/processed_signals/` (174+ files as of 2026-04-05)
3. Pattern analysis in `07_Knowledge/ICP/` (signal_analysis + icp_patterns files)
4. Synthesized archetypes feed [[initiate-arena]] positioning and outreach

## Content Hooks

Effective content angles derived from ICP signals:
- "You don't have a discipline problem. You have a structure problem."
- "Every unfinished project is a loan against your future self-belief."
- "If every week disappears, your environment is built for consumption — not output."
