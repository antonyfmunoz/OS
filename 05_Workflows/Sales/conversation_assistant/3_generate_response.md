# Step 3: Generate Response

## Purpose
Generate the next message to send based on the conversation analysis.

## Instructions

Using the analysis from Step 2, write a response that:

1. **Matches the conversation stage**
   - Cold → build rapport, ask a curiosity question
   - Warm → reflect their pain back, deepen the problem
   - Hot → introduce the reframe or solution naturally
   - Ready → give a clear, low-friction next step (book a call, send a link)

2. **Uses ICP language** — mirror the exact words and phrases the lead used

3. **Does not pitch** unless the lead is at Hot or Ready stage

4. **Is short** — 1-3 sentences max for DM context

5. **Ends with either:**
   - A question (to continue the conversation)
   - A clear CTA (if the lead is ready)

## Tone Guidelines
- Peer-to-peer, never salesy
- Direct but warm
- Confident, not pushy
- Never use: "I'd love to...", "Just wanted to...", "Hope this helps!"

## Output
The final message, ready to copy and send.
