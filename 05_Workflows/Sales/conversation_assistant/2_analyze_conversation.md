# Step 2: Analyze Conversation

## Purpose
Analyze the conversation thread to understand where the lead is and what they need next.

## Instructions

Using the loaded conversation, identify:

1. **ICP State** — which psychological state does this lead match?
   - Ambitious but Stuck
   - Frustrated Drifter
   - Other (describe)

2. **Pain Signals** — what specific pain has the lead expressed?

3. **Objections** — any resistance, hesitation, or pushback detected?

4. **Buying Signals** — any signs of readiness, curiosity about the offer, or urgency?

5. **Conversation Stage**
   - Cold — no rapport yet
   - Warm — rapport established, pain identified
   - Hot — pain confirmed, solution discussed
   - Ready — lead has expressed intent or asked about next steps

6. **Last Message Sender** — did the lead or you send the last message?

7. **Tone** — what is the lead's current emotional tone? (frustrated, hopeful, skeptical, engaged, etc.)

## Output
A structured analysis summary passed to Step 3.
