# Step 4 — Store Insight

## Purpose

Convert analyzed signals into structured knowledge across the OS knowledge database.

## Process

From the ICP analysis generate knowledge entries for relevant categories.

Possible destinations:

### ICP

Store core ICP insights.

Location:  
07_Knowledge/ICP

Example:  
insight_time_drift.md

---

### Messaging

Store language patterns and phrases used by the ICP.

Location:  
07_Knowledge/Messaging

Example:  
language_capable_of_more.md

---

### Objections

Store objections or resistance patterns.

Location:  
07_Knowledge/Objections

Example:  
objection_lack_of_discipline.md

---

### Learnings

Store strategic insights or discoveries.

Location:  
07_Knowledge/Learnings

Example:  
learning_structure_vs_motivation.md

---

### Competitors

If signals mention competitor tools or programs, store insights here.

Location:  
07_Knowledge/Competitors

---

## Result

Signals are transformed into structured knowledge entries across the OS.
