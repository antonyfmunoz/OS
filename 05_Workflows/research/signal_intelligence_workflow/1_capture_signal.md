# Step 1 — Capture Signal

## Purpose

Capture a raw signal from the internet or internal conversations.

Signals can come from:

- Reddit threads
    
- Instagram comments
    
- Quora questions
    
- YouTube comments
    
- Twitter/X discussions
    
- DM conversations
    
- Sales call transcripts
    

## Process

1. Copy the raw signal text.
    
2. Store it as a markdown note in:
    

01_Inbox

3. Give the note a clear name.
    

Example:

reddit_discipline_problem.md

instagram_comment_drift.md

dm_conversation_confusion.md

## Output

Store the raw signal inside:

01_Inbox/raw_signals

Naming format:

signal__.md

Examples:

signal_discipline_reddit.md  
signal_time_wasting_instagram.md  
signal_motivation_dm.md
